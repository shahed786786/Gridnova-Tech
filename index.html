About SynGrid Technologies

At SynGrid Technologies, we develop advanced energy control solutions designed to modernize America's electric grid. Our technologies combine AI-based inverter control, SCADA-integrated fault detection, and predictive energy forecasting—built for real-world deployment across urban, rural, and emergency energy systems.

Founded by Md. Shahiduzzaman Rabbi, a recognized expert in smart power electronics and energy automation, SynGrid is on a mission to deliver scalable, secure, and sustainable infrastructure to support the U.S. clean energy transition.

Our Core Innovations

MPPT-Controlled Z-Source Inverters

Highly efficient solar integration through maximum power point tracking with resilient inverter architecture.

SCADA-Enabled Grid Fault Detection

Real-time fault analytics and isolation using intelligent SCADA networks to prevent outages.

AI-Based Load Forecasting

Smart load prediction models that adapt to grid behavior and optimize energy use.

Cyber-Informed Energy Automation

Protecting grid assets with security-first automation and adaptive threat detection.

Modular Deployment Solutions

Compact and scalable systems tailored for urban microgrids and rural communities alike.
